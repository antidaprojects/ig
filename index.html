About Instageo beta

Instageo is a web service providing you to see realtime photos from over the world. You can write 'tower of pisa' and Instageo will show you all new photos near the Tower of Pisa in Italy. We use Instagram and Google services to provide our functionality.

Developed by Antida software. Send your thoughts and ideas about this project to us: info@antidasoftware.com. Good luck!
